WHY CHOOSE ME & MY SERVICES

EYE CATCHING DESIGN

I pride myself on having an eye for catchy designs that makes the visitor linger a little longer on your website. The longer your customer stays on your website the bigger the chance your business succeeds. I can always share my expertise in this area or bring to life any idea you may have in your mind.

USER EXPERIENCE

In today's world of short attention spans it's important to give your visitor as little reason to leave your website (due to bugs in the code) as possible. Before I hand over the product I will make sure every little detail of the website works the way it was intended to. I want your visitor to have the best user experience they've ever had.

CUSTOMIZABILITY

Creating straightforward and functional websites is nice. But to really stand out you need a website custom-tailored to your needs. In the mock-up process I can help you define what your structure and content will look like so that we don't miss any details.

CLEAN AND LEGIBLE CODE

I pride myself on writing code (from the get-go) that is clean, legible and bug-free so that even an "Average Joe" can almost tell what the code is doing. There is nothing worse than having clunky and cluttered code that is hard to read and de-bug.

FULLY RESPONSIVE

In today's world of myriad of different types of devices you want your website to pop out regardless of which device your visitor is using. I use the amazing Bootstrap 4 class framework to make sure that all the web elements on your page look fantastic no matter whether the visitor is using a table-top, laptop computer, tablet or a smartphone.
